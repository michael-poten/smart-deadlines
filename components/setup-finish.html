It is time to config Smart Deadlines!

To do this, please follow the instructions below.

Step 1

Enter the URL to your iCal calendar in the Power-Up settings.

Optional: Configure your own Smart Deadlines server to upload calculated card appointments as *.ics.

Step 2

Activate Smart Deadlines for one or more list.

Step 3

Estimate a duration for each card of the activated lists.

After the card appointments have been calculated, they are also displayed here.

Step 4

Run the calculation of card appointments.

If other lists are to be calculated first, you can add them as dependencies.
